Welcome to the Myth of the Minotaur

Step back into the ancient world of Crete, where King Minos once ruled, and the legendary labyrinth held the monstrous Minotaur captive. You stand at the entrance to this ancient maze, and now it’s your turn to embark on a journey through myth and legend. Will you be able to navigate through the labyrinth and uncover the mysteries within, or will you fall victim to the traps set by Daedalus?

The labyrinth is not for the faint of heart. To escape, you must prove your wisdom by solving riddles and uncovering hidden clues scattered throughout the pages of this website.

The Labyrinth Puzzle

The labyrinth puzzle is your key to escaping this maze. As you venture deeper, you’ll encounter a series of questions and challenges related to the Minotaur and his myth. Answer them correctly, and you’ll be guided through the twists and turns of the maze. Each question will direct you to navigate the grid, altering your path.

Your Objective: Solve each riddle to move forward, gather clues, and piece together the final answer. Be sure to explore every page carefully, as the clues are hidden in plain sight. Once you’ve completed your journey, click on the "Check My Answer" button in the labyrinth to see if you've successfully escaped!
